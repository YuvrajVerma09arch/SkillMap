About SkillMap

SkillMap is more than just a job portal — it's your personal Career Architect. We exist because the traditional job hunt is broken. Applicants spray resumes into the void, and recruiters drown in unqualified candidates.

Our mission? Bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity using AI.

🎯 Our Story

Born out of frustration during college placements in Ahmedabad, SkillMap was built by students who were tired of generic career advice. We realized three critical problems:

  • Resume Black Holes: Most resumes get rejected by ATS systems before a human even sees them.
  • Unclear Career Paths: Students know where they want to go, but not HOW to get there.
  • Skill-Job Mismatch: Talented people apply to wrong-fit roles, and recruiters miss hidden gems.

So we built tools that solve these exact problems — powered by AI, designed for real people.

🚀 What Makes Us Different

AI-First Approach

Every feature — from resume scoring to interview prep — uses machine learning to give you personalized, actionable insights.

Data-Driven Matching

Our Smart Match algorithm ranks candidates using a heap-based scoring system. Skills, not luck, get you to the top.

Built for Students

We understand the struggle. SkillMap is designed by students, for students and early-career professionals.

Always Free Core Tools

Resume analysis, roadmaps, and mock interviews will always have a free tier. Career growth shouldn't be gatekept.

🛠️ Our Tech Stack

SkillMap is built with modern, production-grade technologies:

Backend: Flask, SQLAlchemy, Werkzeug (Python)
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript
AI/ML: Groq API (LLM inference), Natural Language Processing
Database: PostgreSQL / SQLite
Deployment: Containerized (Docker-ready)

Privacy Policy

At SkillMap, your privacy is not a checkbox — it's a commitment. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

📋 Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide:

  • Account Details: Name, email, password (encrypted), role (seeker/recruiter)
  • Resume Data: Uploaded PDFs, extracted text, skills, experience
  • Career Information: Current role, target role, learning preferences
  • Application Data: Jobs you apply to, interview responses (audio transcripts)

2. Automatically Collected Data:

  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent (via cookies)
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Performance Data: Error logs, API response times (for debugging)

🔒 How We Use Your Data

  1. Core Functionality: To provide AI-powered resume analysis, roadmap generation, interview simulation, and job matching.
  2. Personalization: To customize your dashboard, recommend jobs, and improve match accuracy.
  3. Communication: To send you application updates, feature announcements, and account-related emails (you can opt out).
  4. Security: To prevent fraud, detect spam, and protect against malicious activity.
  5. Improvement: To analyze aggregated usage data and improve our AI models (anonymized).

🛡️ What We DON'T Do:

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We never share your resume or interview responses with anyone except recruiters you explicitly apply to.
  • We never train our AI on your private data without explicit consent.

🤝 Data Sharing

When Recruiters See Your Data:

  • When you apply to a job, the recruiter sees: your name, resume, skills, match score.
  • Recruiters cannot see your interview practice sessions, roadmap details, or other jobs you've applied to.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Groq API: For AI inference (text generation). Prompts are processed but not stored by Groq.
  • Cloud Storage (if applicable): Resume files stored in encrypted AWS S3 / Azure Blob.
  • Analytics (optional): Google Analytics or similar (anonymized, can be disabled).

🔐 Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed using Werkzeug's bcrypt (industry standard).
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption.
  • Database access is restricted and logged.
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates.

✅ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you.
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion (within 30 days).
  • Export: Download your resume, roadmaps, and application history.
  • Opt-Out: Disable marketing emails or analytics tracking.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@skillmap.com

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for authentication (staying logged in) and analytics (understanding feature usage). You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

👶 Children's Privacy

SkillMap is intended for users 16 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If we discover such data, we'll delete it immediately.

🌍 International Users

SkillMap is based in India. If you're accessing from outside India, your data may be transferred to Indian servers (protected under India's IT Act and GDPR-inspired practices).
