About SkillMap

SkillMap is more than just a job portal — it's your personal Career Architect. We exist because the traditional job hunt is broken. Applicants spray resumes into the void, and recruiters drown in unqualified candidates.

Our mission? Bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity using AI.

🎯 Our Story

Born out of frustration during college placements in Ahmedabad, SkillMap was built by students who were tired of generic career advice. We realized three critical problems:

  • Resume Black Holes: Most resumes get rejected by ATS systems before a human even sees them.
  • Unclear Career Paths: Students know where they want to go, but not HOW to get there.
  • Skill-Job Mismatch: Talented people apply to wrong-fit roles, and recruiters miss hidden gems.

So we built tools that solve these exact problems — powered by AI, designed for real people.

🚀 What Makes Us Different

AI-First Approach

Every feature — from resume scoring to interview prep — uses state-of-the-art LLMs to give you personalized, actionable insights.

Smart Matching

Our backend ranks candidates using a custom MaxHeap algorithm. Skills, not luck, get you to the top.

Built for Students

We understand the struggle. SkillMap is designed natively for students and early-career professionals.

Core Tools Free

Resume analysis, roadmaps, and mock interviews have accessible tiers. Career growth shouldn't be gatekept.

🛠️ Our Tech Stack

SkillMap is built with modern, production-grade technologies:

Backend: Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, Celery
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Tailwind, JS, Web Speech API
AI Engine: Groq API (Llama-3-70B-Versatile)
Database: Neon (Serverless PostgreSQL)
Infrastructure: Render Web Services, AWS S3

Privacy Policy

At SkillMap, your privacy is not a checkbox — it's a commitment. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

📋 Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide:

  • Account Details: Name, email, password (encrypted), role (seeker/recruiter)
  • Resume Data: Uploaded PDFs, extracted text, skills, experience
  • Career Information: Current role, target role, learning preferences
  • Application Data: Jobs you apply to, interview responses (audio transcripts)

2. Automatically Collected Data:

  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Performance Data: Error logs, API response times (for debugging)

🔒 How We Use Your Data

  1. Core Functionality: To provide AI-powered resume analysis, roadmap generation, and matching.
  2. Personalization: To customize your dashboard and recommend relevant jobs.
  3. Communication: To send application updates and account-related alerts via Brevo API.
  4. Security: To prevent fraud, detect spam, and protect against malicious activity.

🛑 What We DON'T Do:

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We never share your resume with anyone except recruiters you explicitly apply to.
  • We never train open-source AI models on your private data.

🤝 Data Sharing

When Recruiters See Your Data:

  • When you apply to a job, the recruiter sees: your name, resume, skills, and AI match score.
  • Recruiters cannot see your interview practice sessions or other jobs you've applied to.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Groq API: For AI inference. Prompts are processed temporarily but not permanently stored by Groq.
  • Cloud Services: Render for hosting and Neon DB for secure database storage.
  • Razorpay: Securely processes payment data. We do not store credit card info on our servers.

✅ Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your data, correct inaccuracies, or request total account and data deletion within 30 days. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@skillmap.com.
